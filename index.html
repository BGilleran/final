Hi, I'm Bill

Your Friendly Neighborhood Techie

From a young age, I have been fascinated with all things technology. I love learning about how things work and in turn, teaching others. For years, I have been the goto guy for family and friends alike as a resource for fixing computers, setting up equipment, or navigating dungeons in The Legend of Zelda. My passion for learning and a desire to better myself as computer guru has led to the creation of bgilleran.com

Bgilleran.com will be my virtual workspace, a place for family and friends old and new to reach out for help or even teach me something new. This will be my medium for sharing projects I’m working on, things I’m working toward, and my thoughts on various subject matters involving technology. I’m by no means an expert, but I’m working on it.
